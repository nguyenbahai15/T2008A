-: E-mail Editor Help :-

  • To and Cc:
    To and Cc boxes allow you to enter e-mail addresses of the recepoents to whom to message has to be sent. Recipients be able to see who else was sent the message by looking at the contents of the To and Cc boxer.
  • Subject:
    The Subject box allows you to enter meaningful summary of the message.
  • Message:
    The Message TextArea allows you to enter the text of your message.
