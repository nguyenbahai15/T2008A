NOUNS


A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KIND OF NOUN


Nouns are classsified as follows:

Porper: It is the name of a particular objet, place, animal, idea or thing.
Common: It is the name possessed by things in general.
Collective: It is the name representing a group of things.
Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS


A pronoun is a word used in plece of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN


Pronouns are classified as follow:

Pesonal pronouns: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.
Interrogative pronouns: It stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.
Indefinite pronouns: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.
Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expreddion preceding it is the sentence, and at the same time connects two statemants.
Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specifed by it
Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a nou or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
